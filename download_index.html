Tables/Files for Large Scale Gene Expression Analysis

------ Download Large Scale Expression Data ------


AllDatasetsDownload.tgz --- Microarray, Tiling array and RNAseq data (mapped to the current genome)
MrDataProbeCentric.tgz --- Original Microarray data with spot IDs and Affymetrix calls

------ Experimental Information ------


MrExpTable.csv --- Microarray Experiment Table, with Gene Expression Omnibus IDs, Tissue and Life stage information
GeneAlias.tgz --- All Gene Names (including synonyms) mapped to the current WormBase Gene ID

------ Differentially Expressed Genes ------


ExprClusterTable.csv --- All expression clusters based on chronogram and microarray results
GeneExprCluster.csv --- Expression clusters for each gene
TopoMap.csv --- Stuart Kim Topo Map (mountain) data

------ Tissue and Developmental Life Stages of Gene Expression ------


GeneTissue.csv --- Genes with their tissue distribution based on GFP, immunostaining and in situ hybridization
TissueGene.csv --- Tissues with gene expression based on GFP, immunostaining and in situ hybridization
